Projects

Bare-Metal led matrix control

A low-level programming project focused on working directly with hardware, bypassing traditional operating systems. The project consists on programming barem-metal on a STM32L475 card. It involves setting up and managing the memory, the gpios, uart communication, the led matrice, timers, etc.

Artefact

Design, conception, building and programming logic of autonomous cars, that also have a remote control (on a webpage). The cars use Aruco markers to know were to go and when. They communicate between each other through a server to optimise their course and avoid collisions.

Swarm-rescue

Participation in the Swarm-Rescue project, an extra-curricular project carried out in teams of 5, aimed at designing collaborative robotic solutions for search and rescue missions for people in a building. My role consisted in working on the exploitation of LIDAR data received and the saving and optimization of trajectories. I developed skills in autonomous robotics and collective-oriented programming. This experience also enabled me to strengthen my skills in project management, solving complex problems and working as part of a multi-disciplinary team.

MAD's Website

A website for Make A Difference - the environmental NGO of Télécom Paris.
